GeoBlox Mini Defense

Play in Fullscreen

This game requires a special resolution, so you will need to run it in a new window.

About This Project

Defend the portal in the center of the field against the Blips that seek to destroy the GeoBlox world. Upgrade your Block to defend against waves of Bloops of increasing difficulty. Leak three enemies and it's all over! Can you get the highest score?

Space/Click to shoot. Arrows to move. "E" to open shop when close.
