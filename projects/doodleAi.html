Doodle Guessing AI

About This Project

This is a primitive AI which guesses what you draw based on its "training." Just click to start and then draw what is asked. When your ink runs out, the AI will try to guess what you have drawn. It's kind of like Google's Quickdraw!

This is an old project, but I included it to show some of the projects I used to make. Also, its kind of fun to use! Press the green flag to restart the project. The AI will learn based on what you teach it, but just keep in mind if you leave/refresh the page, its memory will be cleared.
