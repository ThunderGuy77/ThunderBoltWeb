Gravi-Verse

About This Project

Gravi-Verse is an new platformer where you traverse the universe in search of power to alter the past! This game, filled with action, adventure, and more, works on almost all desktop devices and has controller support. You can also play online with friends across the world!

If you are actively interested in the development of this project, I recommend you visit the game on itch.io!
