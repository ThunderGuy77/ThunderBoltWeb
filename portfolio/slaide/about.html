About Slaide

  • Slaide is an AI that learns from you. Slaide knows only the English language and is heavily influenced by your choices.
  • Slaide can be compared to a pet. Just a digital one. With no appearance. Only personality.
  • Slaide is as smart as you are. Slaide doesn't take information from anywhere except the dictionary. Don't expect Slaide to know your math homework.
  • Train Slaide to be your companion. Slaide enjoys your company. Unless you make Slaide dislike you.
  • Use "/" to access certain Slaide commands, like "/stats" or "/help".
